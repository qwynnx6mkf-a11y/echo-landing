Privacy Policy

Welcome to Echo. We are committed to protecting your personal information, especially your memories and AI interaction data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Memory Assets: Chat logs, voice clips, photos, and metadata you upload. These are the foundation for generating "Soul Echoes."
  • Interaction Data: Your conversations with the AI characters.
  • Device Information: Device model and OS version for optimization purposes.

2. AI & Data Usage (Important)

  • Personalized Use Only: Your data is used exclusively to fine-tune your personal AI Agents.
  • No General Training: We DO NOT use your private memory data to train public foundation models.
  • Third-Party Processing: Encrypted data is processed by trusted LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI) solely for generation and is not retained by them.

3. Your Rights

You retain full control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all stored memory fragments at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account or clear memories in settings. This action permanently physically deletes all your data and AI models.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at:
